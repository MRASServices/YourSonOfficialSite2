WELCOME TO THE WORKSHOP

If you know Your Son, you know transparency is important: in our relationship with you, in how we operate, and in the way we conduct ourselves, now and on the road ahead.

Your Son's Workshop helps guide us toward bringing our ideas to life, and offers the community we serve a glimpse of what’s coming down the pipe.

We tell ourselves that this glimpse helps prove that Your Son brings more to the table than just expensive grocery bills and one-word responses when asked, “How was school today?”

In reality, this transparency fosters open dialogue about our ideas-and yours-and the ways in which we can take them beyond just mispelled words on a crumpled napkin from last night's dinner.
